Our Mission

Access to clean and safe hygiene facilities is a fundamental human right. Find My Toilet is designed to bridge the gap between need and availability. Whether you are a local resident or a traveler, our goal is to ensure you never have to worry about finding a restroom when you need one.

Who We Are

We are Team GreenInk, a group of passionate developers dedicated to building smart solutions for urban challenges. This project, Enigma, was born out of a desire to make our cities more livable and tourist-friendly.
