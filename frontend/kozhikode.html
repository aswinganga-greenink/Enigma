Kozhikode

The City of Spices, Sculptures, and Smiles.

A Culinary Paradise

Known historically as Calicut, Kozhikode is the culinary capital of Kerala. From the world-famous Kozhikodan Biryani to the sweet Halwa, the city offers flavors that linger in your memory forever.

Smart City Initiative

As Kozhikode grows into a modern urban hub, infrastructure is key. This Toilet Locator project supports the city's vision of becoming cleaner and more accessible for its growing population and tourists.
